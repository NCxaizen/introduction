Kalka Institute of Research and Advanced Studies (KIRAS), New Delhi was established in 2000 and affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi. KIRAS is managed by an Academic Advisory Board represented by eminent educationists and academicians.


The Kalka Group of Institutions is promoted by Kalka Educational Society. The group has rich experience in imparting quality education for decades. The group is gaining incredible popularity in abroad and has established an Institution of repute in Middle East (Kuwait).
